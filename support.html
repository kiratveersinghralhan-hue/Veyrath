FAQs

How are products made?

Veyrath products are intended to be fulfilled through a print-on-demand partner. Products are printed only after an order is placed.

Will you offer COD?

COD depends on the selected fulfilment/payment partner. For Phase 1, online prepaid orders are recommended.

Can I cancel after ordering?

Cancellation is easiest before the product enters printing. See the cancellation policy for final rules.
