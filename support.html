Support

Straight answers, no fine-print fog.

Because products may be printed after you order, the process differs from buying ready stock. Here is what matters before you purchase.

Are VEYRATH products kept in stock?

Not necessarily. VEYRATH is designed around print-on-demand fulfilment, so a product may begin production only after your order is confirmed.

How do I choose a size?

Use the size information on the fulfilment or Blinkstore product page before paying. Oversized refers to the intended silhouette, but the right labelled size still depends on your measurements and preference.

How long does shipping take?

Production time comes before transit time. Exact estimates depend on the fulfilment partner, destination, service availability, and seasonal volume. Read our shipping policy for the practical range.

Can a custom-printed item be returned?

Change-of-mind returns may be limited because products are made for the order. Damage, defects, or the wrong item should be reported promptly with clear photographs and the order reference.

Can I cancel after ordering?

Contact us immediately. If printing or fulfilment has started, cancellation may no longer be possible. The policy depends on the order stage and partner terms.

Where does payment happen?

The VEYRATH checkout first creates a pending order in Supabase. Card or UPI details must be handled by the configured payment provider, such as Razorpay; they are not stored in this static website. An order is not sent to Qikink until server-side payment verification marks it paid.

A product says “Enquire.” Can I buy it?

That means a live fulfilment link has not been added yet. Send an inquiry and we can share availability; no purchase is promised until a valid checkout option exists.
