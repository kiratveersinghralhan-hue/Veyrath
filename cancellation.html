Cancellation Policy

Since POD products enter printing quickly, cancellation rules must be clear before launch.

Update after final fulfilment partner selection. POD partners may not allow cancellation after production starts.

1. Before production

Orders may be cancelled only if the fulfilment process has not started. Contact support as soon as possible with your order number.

2. After production starts

Once a product enters printing, packing, or dispatch, cancellation may not be possible because the item is made specifically for that order.

3. Failed payments

Orders with failed or incomplete payments are not confirmed. If payment is debited but order is not created, contact the payment provider/support with proof.

4. Veyrath cancellation rights

We may cancel an order due to product unavailability, incorrect pricing/product information, unsupported delivery location, suspected fraud, or fulfilment failure.

5. Refunds for cancelled orders

If cancellation is approved, refund will be processed through the original payment method or available payment provider route.

6. Contact

Email support@veyrath.in [replace] immediately for cancellation requests.
