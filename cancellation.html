Customer care

Cancellation policy.

Act quickly: made-after-order production can begin soon after confirmation.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Requesting cancellation

Contact VEYRATH immediately with your order number and the email or phone used at checkout. A request is not confirmed until VEYRATH accepts it.

Before production

If production has not started, we may be able to cancel the order and initiate a refund to the original payment method, subject to payment-provider processing time.

After production begins

Custom printed and made-after-order products generally cannot be cancelled once production has started, artwork has been processed or the parcel has been packed or dispatched.

Address and product changes

Contact us immediately. Changes are treated like cancellation requests and are not guaranteed once production begins.

Cancellation by VEYRATH

We may cancel an order because of unavailable production, technical pricing error, suspected fraud, failed verification or an undeliverable address. Where payment was captured, an appropriate refund will be initiated.

Refund timing

Bank and payment-provider processing times apply after a refund is initiated. The original payment source determines when the credit appears.
