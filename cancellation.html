Policy / cancellation

Timing matters.

A made-after-order piece can enter production quickly. Cancellation is easiest before that process begins.

PoliciesPrivacyTermsShippingReturnsCancellation
Last updated: 30 June 2026

1. Request cancellation immediately

Use the contact page as soon as you want to cancel and include the order reference, item, and checkout email. A request is not a confirmed cancellation until the fulfilment status is checked and acceptance is communicated.

2. Before production

If the order has not entered printing, procurement, or fulfilment, cancellation may be possible subject to the checkout platform’s process and payment terms.

3. After production starts

Once a custom-printed item has entered production, cancellation or modification may no longer be possible because the item is already being made for that order. Dispatch is not the only cutoff; production may begin before a shipping label exists.

4. Address, colour, or size changes

Treat a change request with the same urgency as cancellation. Contact support immediately, but do not assume a change can be applied after checkout. Creating a second order before receiving confirmation can result in both orders being produced.

5. Refund after accepted cancellation

If cancellation is accepted, the checkout or fulfilment provider will normally return eligible funds to the original payment route. Processing time varies by the payment provider and bank.

6. VEYRATH or partner cancellation

An order may be cancelled if a product cannot be produced, a service area is unavailable, payment is not confirmed, information appears fraudulent, or another genuine fulfilment problem arises. Where payment was captured, the appropriate refund process will be initiated.
