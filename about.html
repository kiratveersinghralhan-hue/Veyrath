Brand story

Born After Dark

Veyrath is a dark luxury streetwear label built around silence, night-drive energy, astrology, and the ambition that grows when nobody is watching.

What Veyrath means

Veyrath is built as a premium Indian streetwear label with minimal front branding and powerful back-print storytelling. The brand starts with oversized T-shirts and grows into hoodies, lowers, and accessories after the first drop validates demand.

The visual language stays dark, clean, mysterious, and premium: black, off-white, grey, deep red, tiny stars, orbit marks, and sharp typography.

Brand rules

  • No copied luxury/car/sports/anime logos.
  • No random meme designs.
  • No low-quality fake hype.
  • Small front print, strong back print.
  • Premium product pages and honest policies.
