Our story

Some ideas only arrive after dark.

VEYRATH is an online-first Indian print-on-demand streetwear label. We are starting small, with oversized T-shirts and a visual language built around the night.

Why VEYRATH

Presence without noise.

Streetwear often races toward more: more graphics, more drops, more urgency. VEYRATH takes the opposite route. We want pieces with enough character to hold a room and enough restraint to stay in rotation.

Our astrology-coded language is not about costumes or predictions. It is a design system—circles, alignments, dark hours, and the strange confidence of knowing your own orbit.

Born after dark is not a time stamp. It is a state of becoming.
