Shipping policy.

Made after order, then moved with care.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Where we ship

VEYRATH primarily serves addresses within India. Serviceability can vary by pincode, product and courier. International delivery should not be promised unless enabled at checkout.

Production before shipping

Some products are printed or made after order. The delivery timeline therefore includes production, quality checks, packing and courier transit. A typical estimate may be shown at checkout or in the order confirmation; it is not a guaranteed delivery date.

Shipping charges

Any shipping fee, free-shipping threshold or COD charge is shown before payment by the active checkout provider.

Tracking

Tracking is shared after the fulfilment partner hands the parcel to a courier. It can take time for the courier’s first scan to appear.

Delays

Production capacity, remote locations, weather, public holidays, courier disruptions and incorrect addresses may cause delays. Contact support if tracking has not moved for an unusual period.

Address accuracy

Customers are responsible for entering a complete name, phone number, address and pincode. Contact us immediately for corrections; changes are not guaranteed after production or dispatch.

Failed delivery

If delivery fails because of repeated unavailability, refusal or an incorrect address, additional shipping charges may apply to resend the parcel. Refund eligibility depends on the circumstances and whether the item was custom produced.
