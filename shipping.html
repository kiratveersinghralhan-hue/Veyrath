Shipping Policy

Clear shipping timelines help payment gateway approval and customer trust.

Update timelines after choosing Blinkstore/Qikink/Printrove or another fulfilment partner.

1. Order processing

Most Veyrath products are intended to be print-on-demand. This means the product is printed only after the order is placed and payment is confirmed.

2. Estimated timelines

Typical processing may take 2–5 business days depending on product type, print queue, and partner availability. Delivery after dispatch may take 3–8 business days depending on location and courier conditions.

3. Shipping areas

We currently plan to ship within India through our fulfilment/logistics partners. Remote locations may take longer or may not be serviceable.

4. Tracking

Tracking details will be shared by email/SMS/WhatsApp or through the fulfilment partner when available.

5. Incorrect address

Customers are responsible for entering accurate name, phone number, complete address, pincode, and landmark. Failed delivery due to wrong details may require extra charges or may not be eligible for refund.

6. Delays

Courier delays, weather, strikes, holidays, local restrictions, or fulfilment partner issues can affect delivery timelines. We will try to support customers where possible.
