1. Production before dispatch

VEYRATH products may be printed only after an order is accepted. A typical production and quality-check period may take approximately 2–7 business days, but the actual timeframe shown by the fulfilment partner or Blinkstore should be treated as the current estimate.

2. Transit estimates

After dispatch, domestic delivery in India may commonly take around 3–10 business days depending on the destination and carrier. Remote areas, service disruptions, peak periods, weather, public holidays, or incorrect address details may add time. These ranges are estimates, not guaranteed delivery dates.

3. Charges and service area

Shipping fees, free-shipping thresholds, cash-on-delivery availability, taxes, and deliverable PIN codes are determined at checkout by the active fulfilment storefront. International shipping should not be assumed unless the checkout explicitly offers it.

4. Tracking

Where tracking is available, it is normally sent to the contact details used at checkout after dispatch. Tracking can take time to show the first carrier scan.

5. Address and delivery problems

Review the name, phone number, PIN code, and full address before payment. Contact support immediately if you notice an error; changes may not be possible after production or dispatch. If a parcel is returned because the address was incomplete, delivery was refused, or repeated delivery attempts failed, re-shipping may require an additional charge.

6. Delays or missing parcels

If tracking has not moved for an unusual period or the parcel is marked delivered but not received, first check with household members, neighbours, reception, and the carrier. Then contact support with the order reference and tracking details so the available partner process can be followed.
