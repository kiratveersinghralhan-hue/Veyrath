Last updated: 30 June 2026 · Applies to this static VEYRATH website

1. Who this policy is for

This policy explains how VEYRATH, an online-operated Indian streetwear brand, handles information submitted through this website. It does not replace the separate privacy terms of Supabase, Qikink, a configured payment provider, shipping carrier, or another service involved in processing an order.

2. Information you choose to provide

We may receive your name, email address, product or order reference, and message when you send an inquiry. If you join the launch list, we receive your email address and the signup source. Approved administrators may use email and password authentication through Supabase; passwords are handled by the authentication provider and are not stored in our product tables.

3. Basic website information

The site may record basic events such as a page view, product quick view, or newsletter signup, together with the page path and limited metadata. We do not intentionally add advertising trackers or collect payment card details on this static site. Your browser also stores preferences such as cookie acknowledgement, whether the launch modal has been shown, and local demo admin data.

4. Why we use information

  • To answer product, order, support, or collaboration inquiries.
  • To send launch notes where you asked to receive them.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the catalogue and website.
  • To understand broad interest in pages and products.

5. Services involved

Website forms and pending orders may be stored in Supabase. Paid orders may be securely transmitted from a Supabase Edge Function to Qikink for print-on-demand fulfilment. A configured payment provider such as Razorpay may process payment separately. GitHub Pages serves the public website files. Each service processes data under its own terms and technical operations.

6. Retention and security

We keep submitted information only as long as reasonably useful for the purpose collected, legal compliance, dispute handling, or security. No internet service can promise absolute security. Administrative access is restricted through authentication and database row-level security when the supplied schema is installed correctly.

7. Your choices

You may ask to correct or delete inquiry or newsletter information associated with your email, subject to legal or operational reasons to retain it. You can clear local preferences through your browser. To stop launch emails, use any unsubscribe method provided in a message or contact us.

8. Children

This website is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. A parent or guardian should contact us if they believe a child submitted personal information.

9. Contact and changes

Use the contact page for privacy requests. We may revise this policy when the site, vendors, or legal obligations change; the date above will be updated.
