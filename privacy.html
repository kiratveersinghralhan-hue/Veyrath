Privacy policy.

Plain-language information about the data needed to operate an online streetwear brand.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Who we are

VEYRATH is an online Indian streetwear brand. This policy explains how information may be handled when you browse the site, join the newsletter, contact support or place an order through a linked checkout.

Information we may collect

We may receive your name, email, phone number, delivery address, order details, product preferences, support messages and basic technical information such as browser type or page activity. Payment providers process sensitive payment information under their own policies; VEYRATH should not store complete card numbers or OTPs.

How information is used

  • To process, fulfil, deliver and support orders.
  • To respond to enquiries, replacements and returns.
  • To send drop announcements when you subscribe.
  • To improve website performance, security and product planning.
  • To meet applicable legal and accounting obligations.

Fulfilment and service providers

Necessary order details may be shared with payment, print-on-demand, logistics, hosting and communication providers solely to provide the requested service. These providers may process data under their own privacy terms.

Static-site local storage

This version can store newsletter signups, enquiries and admin content in the browser’s local storage. Local data remains on that browser unless exported, cleared or submitted to an external service. Do not use the local admin as the only record for live customer operations.

Retention and choices

Information should be retained only as long as needed for its stated purpose or legal requirements. You may request correction or deletion where applicable by contacting VEYRATH support.

Security

No online system is risk-free. VEYRATH uses reasonable controls and avoids requesting passwords, OTPs or complete payment credentials through support messages.

Contact

Use the contact page for privacy requests. Add your final business email and address before publishing this policy.
