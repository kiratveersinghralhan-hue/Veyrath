Privacy Policy

Veyrath respects customer privacy. This policy explains what information may be collected through this website and how it may be used.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone and inquiry details submitted through forms.
  • Newsletter consent and discount access requests.
  • Basic analytics such as page views, product clicks and filter usage.
  • Order information handled by the selected payment or print-on-demand partner.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, send launch updates, improve website experience, manage support and connect customers to product checkout pages.

Cookies

The website uses local storage/cookies for preference memory, cookie consent and lightweight analytics. Users may decline non-essential tracking.

Payments

Payment details are processed by the selected payment gateway or fulfilment checkout provider. Veyrath does not store card or UPI credentials on this static website.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact Veyrath through the Contact page.
