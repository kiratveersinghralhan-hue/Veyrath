Last updated: 1 July 2026

Made-after-order limitation

Many VEYRATH products may be custom printed or made after order. Because they are produced specifically for the order, returns for preference changes, minor colour variation or ordering the wrong size are generally limited unless a product page states otherwise.

Eligible concerns

Contact us promptly if the product arrives damaged, has a manufacturing or print defect, or is the wrong product, colour or size compared with the confirmed order.

What to send

  • Order number and contact details used at checkout.
  • Clear photos showing the complete item and the issue.
  • Photos of the shipping label and packaging.
  • An unboxing video, where available, especially for visible transit damage.

Condition of the item

Keep the product unused, unwashed, unworn and with original packaging while the concern is reviewed. Hygiene marks, washing, alterations or damage after delivery may make a claim ineligible.

Resolution

After review, VEYRATH may offer a replacement, reprint, exchange where available, store credit or refund as appropriate. A replacement normally uses the original confirmed item and delivery details unless otherwise agreed.

Size selection

Oversized fits still have product-specific measurements. Choosing an incorrect size is normally not considered a fulfilment defect. Contact us before ordering if you need guidance.

How to start

Use the contact form with the subject “Return or replacement.” Publish a precise claim window and final support email before accepting live orders.
