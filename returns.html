Return & Refund

For custom/print-on-demand products, returns are usually limited to wrong, damaged, or defective items.

Match final terms to your actual POD partner. Do not promise easy returns if the fulfilment partner does not support them.

1. Print-on-demand nature

Many Veyrath products are printed after order confirmation. Because of this, general returns for change of mind, dislike of design, or wrong size selected by the customer may not be accepted unless the fulfilment partner allows it.

2. Eligible issues

Refund/replacement requests may be considered if the product is damaged, defective, wrong item, wrong size sent by fulfilment partner, or has a clear print/manufacturing issue.

3. Reporting window

Customers should contact us within 7 days of delivery with order number, clear photos, and unboxing video if available.

4. Non-eligible cases

Requests may be rejected for used/washed products, missing tags/packaging where applicable, wrong size selected by customer, minor colour variation, or delayed reporting.

5. Resolution

Depending on partner policy and case review, resolution may include replacement, refund, store credit, or re-shipment.

6. Refund timeline

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method or applicable payment provider. Bank/payment provider timelines may vary.
