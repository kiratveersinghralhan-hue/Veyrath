FAQ

Before you
enter the drop.

Clear answers about the brand, products, fulfilment and customer care.

What is VEYRATH?

VEYRATH is an online Indian streetwear brand focused on oversized silhouettes, dark luxury energy and subtle astrology-coded design.

What does Born After Dark mean?

It represents the ambition and identity built away from attention—the work done when nobody is watching.

What products do you sell?

We begin with oversized T-shirts, followed by hoodies, lowers and accessories.

Are products ready-made or custom printed?

Some pieces may be made after order through print-on-demand fulfilment partners. The relevant product page will explain availability.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery includes production and courier time. Estimated timelines depend on the product, fulfilment partner and destination; see our shipping policy.

Can I cancel my order?

Contact us immediately. Made-after-order products can usually be cancelled only before production begins.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Custom printed products have limited returns. We support eligible cases involving damage, defect, or a wrong item or size sent by us or our fulfilment partner.

What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?

Contact support promptly with your order number, clear photos, packaging images and an unboxing video where available.

How do I choose my size?

Use the chart on the relevant product page and compare it with a garment you already own. Oversized does not mean every size fits the same.

Do you offer COD?

COD availability may vary by product, pincode and checkout method. The payment options shown at checkout are authoritative.

How do I track my order?

A tracking link is shared after production and courier hand-off. Contact support with your order number if it has not arrived.

Can I change the delivery address?

Contact us immediately. Address changes are not guaranteed after production or shipping begins.

Are designs limited edition?

Some drops may be limited. A product page will clearly state when quantity or availability is restricted.

How do I contact support?

Use the VEYRATH contact page and include your order number for order-related questions.
